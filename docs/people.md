## Collaborations

We collaborate closely with other members of Harvard Medical School's
Division of Sleep Medicine, including Dr. Susan Redline, an expert in
the genetic epidemiology of sleep disorders and links to physical
health, who directs the Programs in Sleep and Cardiovascular Medicine
and Sleep Medicine Epidemiology. We also collaborate on a number of
studies with Dr. Jen Pan and others at the Stanley Center for
Psychiatric Research, Broad Institute.
