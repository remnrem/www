Quantitative approaches to sleep, genetics, and brain health   |   Purcell Lab

We apply quantitative approaches to human sleep and genetics as complementary windows onto brain function, health, and psychiatric illness.
Our work is based at the New York State Psychiatric Institute and the Department of Psychiatry at Columbia University Irving Medical Center.
